Discover Kenya's Treasures

Embark on a journey to some of Kenya's most captivating destinations, each offering a unique blend of adventure, relaxation, and natural beauty.

Whether you're drawn to the vibrant cultures of coastal resorts or the awe-inspiring landscapes of national parks, Ziara Heritage invites you to explore the wonders of our homeland.

Dive into the crystal-clear waters of the Indian Ocean, unwind on sandy shores fringed by palm trees, or witness the majesty of wildlife on thrilling game drives through Kenya's renowned national parks. If a destination has piqued your interest, reach out to us to start planning your bespoke journey.

And if you're feeling overwhelmed or torn between choices, our dedicated team is here to ensure every member of your party finds their perfect slice of paradise, all while staying within budget constraints

The Mara: Experience the Majesty of the Maasai Mara

Witness the Great Migration in Africa's Iconic Wilderness

Step into the iconic Maasai Mara National Reserve, where endless plains teem with Africa's most magnificent wildlife. Prepare to be mesmerized by the spectacle of the Great Migration, as millions of wildebeest and zebras traverse the Mara River in a timeless display of nature's grandeur.

SAFARI: Thrilling game drives amidst the Big Five and an array of other wildlife species.

CULTURES: Cultural encounters with the Maasai people, learning about their traditions and way of life.

SIGHTSEEING: Hot air balloon safaris offering breathtaking aerial views of the savannah landscapes

LUXURY: Luxurious safari lodges and camps offering unparalleled comfort and hospitality.

Mt Kenya: Conquer Africa's Second Highest Peak

Embark on an Alpine Adventure

Ascend to new heights with a trek to the majestic summit of Mt Kenya, a haven for adventure enthusiasts seeking unparalleled vistas and rugged landscapes. Traverse through diverse ecosystems, from dense forests to alpine moorlands, and encounter unique wildlife along the way
hiking

TREK: Challenging trekking routes catering to all levels of experience, from novice hikers to seasoned mountaineers.

PANORAMAS: Stunning panoramas of glaciers, valleys, and volcanic peaks from the summit.

WILDLIFE: Opportunities for wildlife spotting, including rare species such as the bongo and mountain nyala.
